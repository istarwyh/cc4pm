No Exit Agent Team Theater runbook

Purpose
- Run a live three-role Agent Team scene inspired by No Exit.
- Do not prewrite a full script. The team lead conducts one round at a time; garcin, ines, and estelle answer only as their own roles.

Files
- stage.json: stage constraints, monitor command, TTS map, round rules.
- roles.json: copyable role prompts for garcin/加尔森, ines/伊内丝, estelle/艾丝黛尔.

1) Create the Agent Team and make sure the inbox exists

Recommended team name: no-exit-live-tts
Required teammate names: garcin, ines, estelle

The monitor reads ~/.claude/teams/no-exit-live-tts/inboxes/team-lead.json. If your Agent Team harness has not created that file yet, prepare it once:

  mkdir -p "$HOME/.claude/teams/no-exit-live-tts/inboxes" && printf '[]\n' > "$HOME/.claude/teams/no-exit-live-tts/inboxes/team-lead.json"

2) Start the theater monitor from the repo root

  node scripts/cc4pm.js theater-monitor --team no-exit-live-tts --summaries "加尔森台词,伊内丝台词,艾丝黛尔台词"

For a no-audio check:

  node scripts/cc4pm.js theater-monitor --team no-exit-live-tts --summaries "加尔森台词,伊内丝台词,艾丝黛尔台词" --dry-run

3) Spawn the three role teammates in another Claude Code session

Give each teammate the matching prompt from roles.json. A concise spawn instruction is:

  You are <garcin|ines|estelle> in the live No Exit Agent Team theater.
  Use the matching role prompt from playground/no-exit-agent-theater/roles.json.
  Do not edit files. Do not write a complete scene. Wait for round prompts.
  When speaking, send exactly one message to team-lead, in character only.

3) Required teammate-message summary markers

Every teammate message must include the speaking role's exact summary marker:
- garcin: 加尔森台词
- ines: 伊内丝台词
- estelle: 艾丝黛尔台词

Good summaries:
- 第1轮 加尔森台词
- 第2轮 伊内丝台词
- 第3轮 艾丝黛尔台词

The monitor filters on those markers. If a line does not speak, check the summary first.

4) Drive the scene live, one round at a time

Round 1: self-defense and demand recognition
Send each teammate:

  Round 1. Speak one in-character line only. Defend your self-image and demand recognition from the room. Do not write for the other roles. Summary must contain your role marker.

Wait until all three lines arrive.

Round 2: respond and attack based on the other two lines
For each teammate, paste the two lines spoken by the other roles and send:

  Round 2. Respond to these two lines from the room: [paste the other two round-1 lines]. Speak one in-character line only. Attack, expose, seduce, or plead from your role's need. Do not write for the other roles. Summary must contain your role marker.

Wait until all three lines arrive.

Round 3: no-exit state
For each teammate, paste the pressure created in round 2 and send:

  Round 3. Show the no-exit state: needing recognition from the other two is exactly what traps you. Speak one in-character line only. Do not resolve the room, open the door, add fire, add a devil, or write for the other roles. Summary must contain your role marker.

5) Stage guardrails

Keep the original pressure of No Exit:
- Second Empire-style locked room.
- No windows.
- No mirror.
- Lamp always on.
- No fire, torture devices, devil, or external rescue.
- The punishment is mutual gaze: each role becomes judge, mirror, jailer, and temptation for the other two.

6) Stop condition

After round 3, stop the live scene or start a new three-round pass with fresh prompts. Do not ask any teammate to summarize the whole play unless you intentionally want to leave theater mode.
